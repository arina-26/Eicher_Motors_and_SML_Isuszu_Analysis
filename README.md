# 📊 Financial Analysis of Eicher Motors (Excel Project)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a detailed financial analysis of **Eicher Motors Ltd.** using Microsoft Excel. The analysis is based on consolidated income statements and common-size financial data over multiple years.

The objective is to evaluate the company’s financial performance, profitability, and cost structure through structured calculations and visual insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze revenue growth trends over multiple years
* Evaluate cost structure (COGS, OPEX)
* Study profitability metrics such as Gross Profit, EBIT, and Net Profit
* Perform common-size analysis for better comparison
* Derive insights for financial decision-making

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Financial Statement Analysis
* Common-Size Analysis
* Pivot Tables & Charts
* KPI Calculation
* Dashboard Visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Company: Eicher Motors Ltd.
* Data Type: Consolidated Income Statement
* Period Covered: FY2022 – FY2025
* Source: Financial statements provided in assignment

---

## 📊 Key Financial Metrics Analyzed

* Total Revenue / Income
* Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
* Gross Profit
* Operating Expenses (OPEX)
* EBIT (Earnings Before Interest & Tax)
* Profit Before Tax (PBT)
* Net Profit (PAT)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Revenue has shown consistent growth from FY2022 to FY2025
* Gross Profit margins improved, indicating better cost efficiency
* COGS percentage has gradually decreased over time
* Operating Profit (EBIT) increased significantly, reflecting strong operational performance
* Net Profit margin improved from ~15.6% (2022) to ~23.5% (2025) 

---

## 📉 Analysis Performed

### 1. Income Statement Breakdown

* Detailed calculation of revenue, expenses, and profits

### 2. Common-Size Analysis

* Each component expressed as a percentage of total revenue
* Helps in comparing financial efficiency across years

### 3. Profitability Analysis

* Gross Margin
* Operating Margin
* Net Profit Margin

### 4. Trend Analysis

* Year-over-year growth patterns in revenue and profits
